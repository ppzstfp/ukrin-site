<section id="about">

<h2>About UKRIN</h2>

<p>

The UK Renal Imaging Network (UKRIN) brings together leading UK renal MRI centres,

uniting physicists, radiologists, and clinicians to develop advanced imaging methods

for kidney disease.

</p>

</section>


<section id="aims">

<h2>Our Aims</h2>


<div class="card">Develop harmonised kidney imaging techniques</div>

<div class="card">Support multicentre clinical research</div>

<div class="card">Expand imaging beyond MRI (CT, ultrasound)</div>

<div class="card">Link kidney disease with whole-body imaging</div>

<div class="card">Drive clinical adoption of imaging innovations</div>


</section>


<section id="projects">
