Meeting Report

14th UKRIN Meeting — Nottingham

The 14th national meeting of UKRIN took place in Nottingham in June 2026, bringing together MR physicists, radiologists, and clinicians from across the network. Sessions covered AFiRM and iBEAT study updates, standardisation of MRI biomarkers, clinical topics including transplant and multiorgan imaging, new AI techniques, and BRC2028 funding opportunities.

Network Growth

Expanding Beyond Kidney MRI

UKRIN is expanding its scope to include whole-body MRI in kidney disease — covering cardiac, brain, muscle, and abdominal imaging in the context of renal conditions, preclinical MRI, ultrasound, PET and CT applications
