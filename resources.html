Tools, Data & Links

Open-source software, data platforms, protocol documents, and links to partner organisations across the international renal imaging community.

Software

Open-Source Tools

💻

UKAT — UKRIN Kidney Analysis Toolbox

A framework for harmonised quantitative renal MRI analysis. Includes a library of image processing and analysis algorithms and a user interface for visualisation, segmentation, and processing. Available on GitHub under UKRIN-MAPS.
📋

XNAT Data Sharing Platform

UKRIN-MAPS built a customised XNAT-based online platform with renal MRI data types, meta-data fields, and quality control modules. Used for multi-site data sharing, SOP management, and phantom QC. Contact the coordinating centre for access.

Measurement Protocols

MRI Biomarker Protocols

Morphology

Total Kidney Volume & Segmentation

Protocols for measuring sinus fat volume, total kidney volume, pelvis volume, and cortex/medulla volumes — the first FDA/EMA qualified imaging biomarker in renal disease (ADPKD, 2015–16).

Perfusion

ASL — Arterial Spin Labelling

Renal blood flow measurement using multi-TI, FAIR, and pCASL approaches. Phase-contrast haemodynamics and angiography protocols are also standardised across UKRIN partner sites.

Oxygenation

BOLD / R2* Mapping

Blood oxygen level-dependent imaging measuring R2* in cortex and medulla. Provides a non-invasive window into renal oxygenation and haemodynamics.

Microstructure

T1 / T2 Relaxometry & Diffusion

T1 mapping (MOLLI 5(3)3), T2 mapping (ME-SE, IR SE-EPI), DWI / IVIM, magnetisation transfer (MTR ON/OFF). Cortex and medulla metrics derived from each.

Links

Partner Organisations

🏭

Kidney Research UK — UKRIN Page

The official UKRIN page hosted at Kidney Research UK, which administers the network and provides links to the nephrology research community and UKKRC.
🌐

RENALMRI.org

The international hub for the renal MRI community. Provides a central point of access and joins up all stakeholders to speed up translation into clinical practice, drug development, and basic research. Next meeting: Uppsala, Oct 11–12, 2027.
📊

ISMRM Renal MRI Study Group

The International Society for Magnetic Resonance in Medicine renal MRI study group, co-chaired by Dr Iosif Mendichovszky (UKRIN interim co-chair). Objectives include promoting standardisation of kidney MRI protocols and analysis methods internationally.
🏥

AFiRM Study — UHDB

Official study information page for the AFiRM multicentre trial (NCT04238299), hosted by University Hospitals of Derby and Burton NHS Foundation Trust.
