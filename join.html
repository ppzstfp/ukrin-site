Who should join?

  • MR physicists working in renal imaging
  • Nephrologists and renal clinicians
  • Radiologists with an interest in kidney disease
  • Early career researchers and PhD students
  • Industry partners in MRI and renal health
  • International collaborators in renal MRI

What happens next?

After registering you will be added to the UKRIN mailing list. You will receive:

  • Invitations to national UKRIN meetings
  • Network newsletters and study updates
  • Notifications of funding calls (NIHR, MRC, Horizon)
  • Access to shared protocols and UKAT software

Questions?

Contact the UKRIN coordinating team via Kidney Research UK or find us at renalmri.org.
